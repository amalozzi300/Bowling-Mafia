Rules

USBC rules apply unless otherwise stated

Participants must be USBC sanctioned prior to the beginning of practice

USBC sanction cards will be available at check-in for $25

Youth bowlers are eligible to participate and will receive all winnings as SMART money

Participants will use the highest of the following averages:

- Highest 22-23 season league average (minimum 15 games bowled)

- Highest 21-22 season league average (minimum 15 games bowled)

- Highest 2022 Summer league average (minimum 15 games bowled)

Participants with no qualifying average will be entered using a scratch 230 average

Sport shot averages will be converted to house shot using the approved USBC conversion chart

Bowlers will move one pair to the right at the conclusion of each game
